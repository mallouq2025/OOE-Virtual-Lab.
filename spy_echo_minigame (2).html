How to play:
The wireless signal bounces off the metal wall, creating a standing wave A(x) = 2A₀|sin(kx)|.
Signal is maximum at antinodes (x = λ/4, 3λ/4, ...) and zero at nodes (x = λ/2, λ, ...).
Place your interceptor at an antinode to capture the strongest signal and decrypt the password!
